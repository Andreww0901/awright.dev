New Beginnings

- (24/03/2024)


- After loosing my previous website to a catastrophic failure a few months ago and my domain name pointing to nothing ever since, I thought it was probably time to get a page back up and running. I've completely started from scratch this time and its hosted via GitHub pages. No longer do I have to worry about programming routes in Flask or the innevitable self hosting issues that come with modWSGI. This is in no way a slight against Flask, I personally think its great to use and quite powerful for what it does, but for a simple static site such as this, I simply had no need for it.

Much like with my previous site, I want to post periodic updates to this page in the hopes that it will sort of become a natural reflection of my contributions to society. It may also become a place where I just rant and talk about random stuff that I find interesting!

As you can see from the giant banner at the top of the page, reminiscent of webpages from the early 2000s, https://awright.dev is still very much under construction and I hope to add more to it over the coming months! So check back soon for updates!
